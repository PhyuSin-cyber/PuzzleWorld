🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

✅ No personal data collected 🚫 No tracking cookies 📊 No analytics 💾 All data stays on your device

📊 Game Statistics

Your game progress (completion times, move counts, history) is stored only in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

🚫 No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics. No tracking cookies are used. Your privacy is fully respected.

🔌 Third-Party Services

Puzzle images may be loaded from external sources, but no personal data is shared. No analytics or advertising services are used.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our website is safe for all ages. We do not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

⚙️ Your Control

You can clear all saved data anytime using the "Clear All Statistics" button on the Statistics page, or by clearing your browser's storage.

📝 Changes to This Policy

Any policy changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of PuzzleWorld constitutes acceptance of any changes.

📬 Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our About page and send us a message.
