🧩 About PuzzleWorld

Your ultimate destination for online jigsaw puzzles

Our Story

PuzzleWorld was born from a simple idea: bring the joy of traditional jigsaw puzzles to the digital world. What started as a small project has grown into a community of puzzle enthusiasts who love the challenge and satisfaction of completing beautiful puzzles.

We believe that puzzles are more than just games – they're a way to relax, exercise your brain, and experience the satisfaction of bringing order from chaos. Our mission is to provide the best online puzzle experience with smooth gameplay, beautiful images, and meaningful statistics to track your progress.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide a relaxing yet challenging puzzle experience that helps people unwind, improve cognitive skills, and have fun – all in one place.

Our Vision

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is PuzzleWorld free to play?

Yes! PuzzleWorld is completely free. All puzzles and features are available at no cost.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed. Your statistics are saved locally in your browser.

Can I play on my phone?

Absolutely! PuzzleWorld is fully responsive and works great on all devices.

How are statistics saved?

We use your browser's local storage to save your game history and achievements.

Will you add more puzzles?

Yes! We're constantly working on adding new images and features.

Can I suggest a feature?

We'd love to hear your ideas! Contact us through our feedback form.
